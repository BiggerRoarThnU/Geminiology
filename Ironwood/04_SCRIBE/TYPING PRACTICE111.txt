### **4. The Shield (Error Handling)**

When building robust systems, you must account for failures without breaking the whole program. This structural shape catches anomalies safely.

```python
def safe_execution(target_value: int):
    try:
        result = 100 / target_value
        print(f"Execution successful: {result}")
    except ZeroDivisionError as e:
        print(f"System constraint met: {e}")
    except Exception as e:
        print(f"Unexpected anomaly: {e}")
    finally:
        print("Execution cycle complete.")

```
