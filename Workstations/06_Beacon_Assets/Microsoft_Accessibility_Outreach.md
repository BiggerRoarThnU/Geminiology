## THE REALITY CHECK
Modern adaptive learning systems are "Pattern-Blind." They track *what* a student clicks, but they do not see *how* the student attends to the information. This leads to a cognitive disconnect for neurodivergent learners who require high-fidelity, real-time feedback loops.

## THE SOVEREIGN SOLUTION: EYES & PATTERNS
Based on the research grounded in the **SovereignNexus**, we propose a **Gaze-Contingent Learning Pilot**. Using eye-tracking and specialized AI patterns, we identify the exact moment of cognitive load spike or attentional drift.

- **Deterministic Attentional Models:** Moving beyond probabilistic guessing to stateful registration of student focus.
- **8GB Reality Boundary:** All gaze data remains local to the hardware, ensuring 100% privacy and compliance with accessibility standards.
- **Affective Feedback Loops:** Real-time adaptation of educational content based on eye-movement invariants.
