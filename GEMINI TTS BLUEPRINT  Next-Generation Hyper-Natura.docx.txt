вЂ‹The future of TTS is not just generating audio, it's about linguistic interpretation.

вЂ‹Hybrid Generative Models: Architectures like ConfoGAN leverage the quality of diffusion principles specifically for expressive features (style conditioning) while relying on the speed of efficient GAN-based frameworks (like HiFi-GAN components) for instantaneous, real-time waveform synthesis.

вЂ‹TTScore-pro (Prosody): Measures alignment with the intended rhythm and intonation.
