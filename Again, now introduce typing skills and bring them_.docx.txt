The Dual-Drive Philosophy: Mind and Fingers
The goal is to make the act of writing code as effortless as possible. When your fingers know the patterns, symbols, and keywords of Python and TensorFlow without conscious thought, your mind is free to focus entirely on the logic of the AI model you are building.
In this framework, each phase has a core AI Goal and a corresponding ⌨️ Typing Synergy section. The typing drills are not random; they are designed to be deliberate practice, using the exact code syntax you are learning at that moment.

Phase 4: Advanced Application & IDE Velocity 🌟
AI Goal: Integrate the Gemini API, deploy models with TensorFlow Lite, and explore high-performance libraries like JAX.
⌨️ Typing Synergy:
Focus: Move beyond just typing characters to using the keyboard to command your entire coding environment. This is the final stage of fluency, where you use keyboard shortcuts to manipulate code, reducing your reliance on the mouse.
Practice Drills:
Mastering Your IDE (Colab/VS Code): Dedicate 10 minutes each day to practicing only shortcuts.
Ctrl + / (or Cmd + /) to comment and uncomment lines of code.
Tab and Shift + Tab to indent and un-indent blocks.
Ctrl + D (or Cmd + D) to select the next occurrence of a word, allowing you to edit multiple instances at once.
Alt + Up/Down Arrow (or Option + Up/Down) to move entire lines of code up and down.
